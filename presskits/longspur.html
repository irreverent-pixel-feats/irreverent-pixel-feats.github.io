Description

Created in the spirit of the graphical adventure games of the 1990s, The Vigilante Proclivities of the Longspur is set in the not too distant future, where there are superpowered humans and artificial lifeforms are just in their infancy. Marlowe Reed is neither, he’s a clinical psychologist trying to piece together the broken lives that so many of those without superpowers seem to always have, until one day when he experiences the call himself. Will he become a part of the problem or will he continue hiis work from a fresh angle?

History

The Vigilante Proclivities of the Longspur is created out of the developers love for classic 2D point and click adventures, comic books and 90s animations styles.
